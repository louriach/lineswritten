Systems thinking

Driving Dolly, our design system

One of my first initiatives, in fact I stated my desire at interview stage, was to establish a design system of sorts to drive our efforts.

Given my technical background, I was fully aware that in order for us to employ a rapid and efficient approach to product development, we needed a component-first mindset from design through product and finally tech.

Key milestones

  • –Budgeting, pitching and delivering system/s used by ~10 colleagues
  • –Establishing an extensive Atomic series of libraries and guidelines
  • –Onboarding product, design and tech teams with workshops
  • –Wholesale tool migration and consolidation to Figma

Why call it Dolly? Given we're in the travel business, and you can obtain everything you want on a flight from the 'Dolly trolley', I felt the name would be perfect for a holistic system.
